# 🎓 AI-Based Student Dropout Prediction and Counselling System

An AI-powered web application that predicts students who are at risk of dropping out by analyzing academic and behavioral data. The system provides personalized counselling suggestions and enables teachers and administrators to take early action to improve student success and retention.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

The AI-Based Student Dropout Prediction and Counselling System is designed to help educational institutions identify students who may be at risk of dropping out. The system evaluates multiple academic and behavioral factors such as attendance, examination marks, assignment completion, internal assessment scores, backlog history, class participation, motivation level, stress level, previous failures, and teacher remarks.

Using a weighted risk evaluation model, the application calculates a risk score and classifies students into three categories:

- 🟢 Low Risk
- 🟡 Medium Risk
- 🔴 High Risk

Based on the predicted risk level, the system generates counselling recommendations that help teachers and mentors support students before academic issues become serious.

## 📊 Workflow

1. Student records are added to the system.
2. Academic performance data is uploaded.
3. The AI risk model evaluates student performance.
4. A risk score is generated.
5. Students are categorized into Low, Medium, or High Risk.
6. Personalized counselling suggestions are provided.
7. Teachers and HODs monitor progress through dashboards.
